Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

At Arf!, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our pet care management app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Pet information (name, breed, age, weight, photos)
  • Health records and medical information
  • Activity logs and feeding schedules
  • Reminder settings and preferences
  • Journal entries and notes

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics and crash reports
  • Location data (only when you grant permission for location-aware reminders)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Arf! app functionality
  • Send you important reminders and notifications
  • Improve our app features and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

Data Storage and Security

Local Data Storage

All your pet data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We do not upload or store your personal information on our servers.

Permissions We Request

Notifications

We request notification permissions to send you important pet care reminders, vaccination due dates, and other time-sensitive alerts.

Location Services

Location permission is optional and used only to enhance location search results for setting location-aware reminders. We do not track your location continuously.

Photo Library

Photo library access allows you to add and manage pet photos within the app. Photos are stored locally on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data remains on your device and is not shared with external services unless:

  • You explicitly choose to share data (e.g., exporting health records)
  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Modify: Edit or update your pet information at any time
  • Delete: Remove pets, records, or the entire app data
  • Export: Export your data in a readable format
  • Control Permissions: Manage app permissions through iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

Arf! is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: apps@kantoprojects.com

This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 2024 and applies to all users of the Arf! app.
